Sports Gear Rental Hub

Comprehensive Platform for Renting Sports Gear

Project Overview

Sports Gear Rental Hub is a comprehensive platform that connects sports equipment owners with individuals looking to rent gear for their sporting activities. The platform allows users to browse listings, book equipment, and make payments securely.

Key Features

  • Equipment Listing

    Browse available sports gear and equipment

  • Secure Booking

    Book equipment securely with payment integration

  • User Reviews

    Read and leave ratings and feedback for gear

  • Availability Calendar

    Check equipment availability before booking
